## GDPR Commitment: User Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: October 23, 2024

At SmartLeadMagnet, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with GDPR. This page outlines the steps for deleting your data from our systems, in line with GDPR requirements.

### How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, please email us at the following address:

- **Email:** [support@smartleadmagnet.com](mailto:support@smartleadmagnet.com)

### Information to Include in Your Request

When submitting your data deletion request, ensure that the following details are provided to help us process it efficiently:

1. **Subject Line:** "Data Deletion Request"
2. **Your Name:** The full name you registered with SmartLeadMagnet.
3. **Registered Email Address:** The email associated with your SmartLeadMagnet account.
4. **Reason (Optional):** You may include a reason for your request, though it is not required.

### What Happens Next?

Upon receiving your request, we will follow this procedure:

1. **Acknowledge Receipt:** You will receive an acknowledgment of your request within 24 hours.
2. **Verification:** We may contact you to verify your identity to ensure the protection of your data.
3. **Data Deletion:** Once verified, we will proceed with deleting your data. This process will typically be completed within 3-5 business days.
4. **Confirmation:** After the data is deleted, you will receive a confirmation email from us.

### Important Information

- Deleting your data is permanent and cannot be reversed. Ensure that you back up any important information before requesting data deletion.
- For any questions or further assistance, feel free to contact us using the email address listed above.

### Contact Us

For questions about our GDPR compliance or data privacy practices, please contact us at [support@smartleadmagnet.com](mailto:support@smartleadmagnet.com).
